About PlasticFreeStorage

We help people find the best plastic-free alternatives for food storage, water bottles, travel mugs, and lunch boxes. With growing research on microplastics in our food and water, switching to glass, stainless steel, and silicone is one of the simplest health improvements you can make.

Our Mission

To make it easy for anyone to find safe, durable, plastic-free products for their kitchen and daily life. We research, compare, and recommend products based on quality, durability, and value — not brand hype.

How We Make Money

We use affiliate links throughout our guides and articles. When you purchase a product through our links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps us keep the site running and continue creating free content.

Our Standards

We only recommend products we believe in. Every recommendation is based on extensive research, expert reviews, and real user feedback. We never accept payment for positive reviews.
