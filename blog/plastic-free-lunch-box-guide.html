Plastic-Free Lunch Box Guide

Published: April 2026 | Reading time: 7 min

Packing lunch in plastic containers means your food sits in contact with plastic for hours — especially problematic for acidic foods (tomato sauce, citrus) and warm foods that accelerate leaching. Here are the best stainless steel alternatives.

Why Stainless Steel for Lunch?

  • Zero chemical leaching, even with acidic or hot foods
  • Dents but doesn't crack — lasts years longer than plastic
  • Easy to clean, no staining or odor retention
  • 100% recyclable at end of life
